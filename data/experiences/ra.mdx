# Research Assistant Experience at University of Toronto 

As a Research Assistant for Computer Science Education under Dr. Tinging Zhu and Dr. Andrew Petersen, I focused on investigating the impact of storytelling visualization techniques on understanding Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA). This research aimed to explore innovative methods to enhance learning outcomes and engagement in computer science education. We had a presentation at SURF 2023!

## Key Responsibilities
- Developed engaging storytelling scripts for visualizing concepts in Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA), enhancing understanding and retention among learners.
- Conducted voice acting to effectively convey narratives and explanations, improving presentation skills and engagement in educational content delivery.
- Utilized statistical analysis to evaluate the effectiveness of storytelling techniques in enhancing learning outcomes and student comprehension.
